PHYSICAL & MEDICAL PARAMETERS IN INDIAN ARMY

BPET

The Battle Physical Efficiency Test (BPET), which is a series of physical tests is used to test the physical fitness of serving soldiers and officers to perform military tasks.

PPT

The Physical Proficiency Test (PPT) is designed to assess the physical fitness and endurance of serving soldiers and officers. It is an essential part of maintaining operational readiness and ensuring that personnel meet the physical standards required for military duties.

AME

The Annual Medical Examination (AME) assesses your overall health and fitness level through a series of physical and mental evaluations such as height and weight measurements, hearing and vision exams, urine and blood tests, and drug and alcohol tests.
